Sharon Lawrence

CEO & Co-Founder, Joe Finance

Sharon Lawrence is a visionary leader with a strong background in International Business and Finance. As the CEO & Co-Founder of Joe Finance, he has been instrumental in shaping the company into a leading financial solutions provider in the UAE.

Sharon has played a pivotal role in expanding Joe Finance’s offerings, which include personal loans, business loans, debt management, and financial consultation. His dedication to excellence in the finance sector has helped Joe Finance grow into a trusted name.

In addition to his leadership at Joe Finance, Sharon is also the Co-Founder of Emir Gold Refinery Limited, a prominent name in the precious metals industry.

Under his leadership, Joe Finance has built a reputation for transparency, efficiency, and customer-first service. He is committed to providing tailored financial solutions, ensuring that individuals and businesses receive the best support to achieve their financial goals.
